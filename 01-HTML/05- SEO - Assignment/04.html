Information about semantic tag

Semantic HTML, also known as semantic markup, refers to the use of HTML tags that convey the meaning—or semantics—of the content contained within them By adding semantic HTML tags to your pages, you provide additional information that helps define the roles and relative importance of the different parts of your page For example, tags like <header>, <article>, and <footer> are semantic HTML tags. They clearly indicate the role of the content they contain.
